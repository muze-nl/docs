# Concept: Pod

Pods, from “Personal Online Data Store”, are the user data storage medium used by Solid.

A pod is stored under the control of the user who owns it, either on their own hardware or on a pod provider of their choosing. The owner of a pod controls which applications can access it, and what resosurces those applications can access.

Pods appear online through the Solid protocols, and can be accessed through a URL. They take the form of a simple online filesystem, from which files can be retrieved, to which files can be sent, and in which files can be updated.
